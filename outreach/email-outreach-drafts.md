## Tips

- **Send emails Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM.** That's when business owners and office managers actually read email.
- **Follow up by phone 3 business days after emailing.** One call. Brief. "Hi, I sent an email earlier this week about tile subcontracting — just wanted to make sure it landed."
- **Don't attach photos in the first email.** Keep it text-only so it doesn't get caught in spam. Offer to send photos in a reply.
- **After any positive response:** Send 4–6 portfolio photos in a follow-up email. Lead with the best shower. Include a shot of Kevin working.
- **Track responses.** Simple spreadsheet: Company | Date Sent | Response | Follow-up Date | Status.
