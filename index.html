What Boulder Homeowners Are Saying
Research from Reddit r/boulder, Nextdoor, and Facebook groups — May 2026
What They're Looking For
Reliable, insured pros who communicate. Someone who coordinates well with other trades, handles permits, finishes on time. They post recommendations constantly — when they find someone good, they share.
What They Complain About
"Boulder Tax" (inflated quotes), contractors who ghost, crews who can't take smaller jobs, poor communication during work. Top-tier installers are booked months out. This is the gap Kevin fills.
What They Pay
Full bathroom remodels: $20k–$30k+ is common. Handyman rates: $75–$135/hr. Tile labor: $30–$40+/sq ft. Boulder is 18% above national average — and homeowners know it and accept it.
