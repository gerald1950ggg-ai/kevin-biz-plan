## Market Pricing (Colorado / Boulder)

- **Tile install:** $12–$47/sq ft (Colorado average, 18% above national)
- **Labor rate:** $50/hr average skilled trade
- **Full bathroom remodel tiers:**
  - Basic: $8k–$15k
  - Mid-range: $15k–$35k (most Boulder primary baths land here)
  - High-end: $35k–$80k+
- **Boulder County mid-range:** $65–$95/sq ft (sustainability-focused, LEED requirements common)
- **ROI:** Boulder bathroom remodels return 88–95% at resale (highest in CO)
- **Peak summer demand:** 15–20% premium (we're entering that window now)
