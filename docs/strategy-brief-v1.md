## The Opportunity

Boulder's tile/remodel market is genuinely undersupplied. The best-reviewed local tile installer (Jayhawk Tile) is booked **months out**. People on r/boulder, Nextdoor, and Facebook groups routinely post begging for reliable trades. The #1 complaint: good contractors are impossible to find and half ghost after the quote.

Kevin's edge:
- **30 years of high-end tile** — certifiably above the local competition
- **Wedi PRO Certified #00599** — a direct credential that tile shops and designers care about
- **Schluter/KERDI/DITRA systems** — what every serious bathroom remodeler wants
- **Full bathroom remodels, head to toe** — not just a tile guy, a complete solution

Boulder homeowners pay **$20k–$30k+** for a mid-range bathroom remodel. Kevin's labor on a project like that is $5k–$12k. One job this week is entirely achievable.
